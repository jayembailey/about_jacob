"Here's the thing..."

Originally from Montgomery, AL, Jacob moved to NYC at age 20 to pursue a career in rap music. After 10 years of bartending in the city that never sleeps and brewing beer professionally at Finback Brewery in Queens, NY, Jacob traveled down the Eastern United States for three months in a 1984 Toyota Dolphin motorhome, which was subsequently destroyed in an auto accident about 10 minutes after entering the limits of Tallahassee, FL. After a brief decompression in North Florida, Jacob landed in the coastal town of St. Petersburg, FL, where he resides to this day.

Hobbies and interests

You can catch usually catch Jacob at the local kava bar plugging away at his projects for the Coding Temple Sofware Engineering bootcamp. When he can find the time for a break, he usually heads to the climbing gym to send some routes or pump some iron. He also enjoys kiteboarding, and he's been getting into running, though his eternal flame burns for his 1984 Fuji Tour IV bicycle. Catch him blazing up the Pinellas Trail on the way to work at The Wheelhouse or logging the miles up through the Gulf Coast.
